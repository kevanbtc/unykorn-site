System Overview

The XAUFTH/uUSD platform enables treasury-backed minting of gold-pegged stablecoins, with full lifecycle management including minting, bridging, trading, and redemptions. All operations are Safe-gated, timelocked, and auditable for institutional clients.

Unykorn-1: Sovereign Gold-Backed DeFi, Audit-Verified.
